Regular Math Curriculum

Subjects

We can focus on any math subject that your child needs help with.

What I Offer

I am an academic resouce that will be in your child's corner to help them succeed. I have years of experience centered around empathy and understanding and this has been key to my students' success.

Math doesn't have to be hard—it just needs to be taught the right way. Let's make it straightforward, easy, and even fun!

Algebra

From solving equations to graphing functions, algebra is all about finding the right tools and techniques. I teach straightforward methods that work and help students feel comfortable and capable, no matter the problem.

Geometry

Shapes, angles, proofs—geometry doesn't have to feel like a puzzle. I'll break down concepts step by step, so students can see how it all fits together. They'll gain confidence and skills to tackle anything from transformations to coordinate geometry, all in a judgment-free, fun learning environment.

Pre-Calculus

Precalculus can feel like a leap, but it's all about preparation. Together, we'll build a solid foundation so students feel ready to handle calculus with ease. We'll focus on simplifying challenging topics like sequences, series, and functions, and making them fun along the way.

Calculus

Calculus doesn't have to be overwhelming. I'll guide students through derivatives, integrals, and limits with clear explanations, plenty of practice, and relatable examples that make abstract ideas feel real. Together, we'll make calculus a subject they can conquer.
