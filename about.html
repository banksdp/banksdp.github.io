My name is Banks, I grew up in the suburbs of the SF Bay Area 🌁 in California in Marin County. Living in this area, I naturally enjoy time outdoors, biking, running, and hiking ⛰ with my wife Oda and my doodle Leo.

I attended the Ross School where I found that I enjoyed reading the stats page on the news papers instead of the fairy tales I had assigned by my English teacher.

In High School I played baseball as a pitcher most of the time, but the other times I was taking advanced math classes and spending time with friends riding my BMX bike.

I got my degree on Applied Mathematics with emphasis in Computer Science in 2017 and since then I have been tutoring Mathematics for a living.

Another one of my passions is cryptocurrency. I created a decentralized cryptocurrency named BanksCoin (BKC) that is a low float, high difficulty mineable crypto-commodity.
